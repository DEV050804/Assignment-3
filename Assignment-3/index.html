Home

M&M's are colorful button-shaped chocolates, each coated with a hard candy shell. They are produced in various colors and have become popular worldwide as a snack and in baking.

In 1911, Frank C. Mars founded Mars Incorporated, a small confectionery business in Tacoma, Washington. When it came time for Frank to retire, he groomed his son to take his place, provided he spends some time overseas to learn how to start his own business.

Facts

Anyone who considers themselves any kind of candy connoisseur has heard the phrase before and most likely indulged in the candy it’s describing. Since being released to the masses in the 1940s, M&M’s have been a staple of the candy lovers' diet.
